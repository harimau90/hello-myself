Bloom of Magical Flower

  • By:
  • (GM)Evil
  • Date:
  • 2020-04-26

Many doctors through out the land have waited for this time with anticipation. The rare magical flower that grows once every 50 years is scheduled to bloom once more. The flowers when made into medicine have the power to promote rapid recovery from wounds. The medicine made from this flower is also the only known cure for the rare but deadly Bone Marrow Disease. Unfortunately the flowers are very fragile and only a few plants are ever found in very dangerous areas. As each plant blossoms only a single flower, this makes it a rare and highly coveted herb. The Ruthless, who suffers from the Bone Marrow Disease, has announced his intentions to trade his fabled Willow Sword to anyone who will give him the medicine made from the Magical Flower. Chu Chu is also known to seek the flower as her father; Yu Yue continues to suffer from his amputated arm. Player who is interested in helping them to find the rare magical flower must act at once because many other powerful figures in the martial arts world also seek the Magical Flower. 
Rewards:
First 10 persons complete quest to help the Ruthless10 Willow Sword
First 10 persons complete quest to help the Yu Yue10 Kirin Armor
The rest:100K exp
Date : 26/4/2020 10PM GMT +8
